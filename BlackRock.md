# BlackRock – Data Analyst / Data Science

In the first round, the discussion was mainly centered around my projects. There was a detailed conversation on my loan prediction project, including feature selection and BFSI-related concepts like NPA, DPD, and credit systems. The interviewer also asked about precision-recall trade-offs from a business perspective.

We also discussed the use of LLMs in finance, including data privacy concerns and approaches like anonymization and RAG pipelines. The conversation was quite engaging and lasted around 50–60 minutes.

However, since I was one of the last candidates to be interviewed, I did not get a chance to appear for the next round as the company had already finalized candidates. This taught me not to over-prioritize one company at the expense of others.
