# HiLabs – Data Scientist

The recruitment process for HiLabs started abruptly with a Data Structures and Algorithms screening. In the very first round, the interviewer asked me to share my screen, open an online Python compiler, and solve a specific DSA problem that he dictated on the spot. 

While I managed to reach a solution, I struggled to fully optimize the logic to the expected time complexity within the tight time frame. Unfortunately, I was rejected straight away after this round. From what I heard from other candidates who advanced, the process subsequently involved a dedicated machine learning round focusing on resume projects, followed by an HR segment. This brief, intense experience taught me that even for advanced Data Science roles, strong and quick algorithmic implementation is non-negotiable.
